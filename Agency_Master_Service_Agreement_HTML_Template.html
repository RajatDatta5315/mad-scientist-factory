Agency Master Service Agreement

1. Introduction

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of Month Day, Year (the "Effective Date"), by and between Your Agency Name, a Type of Entity (e.g., LLC, Corporation), with its principal place of business at Your Agency Address ("Agency"), and Client Company Name, a Type of Entity (e.g., LLC, Corporation), with its principal place of business at Client Company Address ("Client").

Agency and Client may be referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

2. Purpose of Agreement

This Agreement sets forth the general terms and conditions under which Agency will provide various marketing, design, development, and/or other related services to Client. Specific details regarding the scope of work, deliverables, timelines, and fees for each project will be outlined in separate Statements of Work ("SOWs") or project proposals, which shall incorporate the terms of this Agreement by reference.

3. Services

Agency agrees to provide services as described in mutually agreed-upon SOWs. Each SOW will include, but not be limited to:

  • Description of Services
  • Deliverables
  • Project Timeline
  • Fees and Payment Schedule
  • Specific responsibilities of each Party

4. Fees and Payment

Client agrees to pay Agency the fees specified in each SOW. Unless otherwise stated in an SOW, all invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days from the invoice date. Late payments may incur interest at a rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

5. Term and Termination

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in full force and effect until terminated by either Party. Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon thirty (30) days written notice to the other Party. In the event of termination, Client shall pay Agency for all services performed and expenses incurred up to the termination date, subject to any prior written agreements for specific project cancellation clauses.

6. Confidentiality

Both Parties agree to keep confidential all non-public information received from the other Party during the course of this Agreement ("Confidential Information"). This obligation includes, but is not limited to, business plans, financial information, client lists, and technical data. This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

7. Intellectual Property

Upon full payment of all fees due under an SOW, Client shall own all rights, title, and interest in and to the final deliverables created by Agency specifically for Client under that SOW. Agency retains the right to use such deliverables for promotional and portfolio purposes unless otherwise agreed in writing within an SOW.

Agency retains ownership of any pre-existing intellectual property, tools, methodologies, or generic templates used in the creation of deliverables, granting Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use such elements solely in conjunction with the deliverables.

EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY PROVIDED IN THIS AGREEMENT, AGENCY DISCLAIMS ALL WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING, BUT NOT LIMITED TO, IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY AND FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

THE TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY OF AGENCY TO CLIENT FOR ANY CLAIM ARISING OUT OF OR IN CONNECTION WITH THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID BY CLIENT TO AGENCY UNDER THE APPLICABLE SOW DURING THE six (6) months IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM.
